Addon, to prevent the opening of tabs with urls, which are already opened (in the same context) Short Demo Video: https://github.com/igorlogius/dont-open-duplicate-tabs/assets/67047467/03d8ccd4-6dc7-4944-9ca9-b2a4acf8b69c Usage/Features:
  • Enable/disable via the toolbar icon
  • Whitelist to allow duplications of URLs
  • Option to change focus to already open tab
  • Option to disable/enable removal notifications
